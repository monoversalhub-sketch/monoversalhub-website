🆘 Product One
SOVR
Community SOS & Emergency Response Platform

When something goes wrong, the help you need is often already nearby — you just can't find it fast enough. SOVR closes that gap by connecting people in crisis with individuals and organizations around them who have the capacity to respond.

  • ✓
    Raise an SOS from anywhere — a user in trouble signals their emergency type and location instantly.
  • ✓
    Smart proximity matching — nearby users with the right capacity are notified immediately.
  • ✓
    Real-time rescue options — victims see available helpers ranked by proximity and capability.
  • ✓
    Dual-role users — every user is both a potential victim and a potential rescuer.

📐 Product Two
FYTD
AI Measurements & Body Fit Intelligence Tool

Bad fit is a universal frustration — clothes that don't fit right, tailors who guess, measurements taken and forgotten. FYTD is an intelligent measurements companion that stores, analyzes, and makes sense of body data.

  • ✓
    Intelligent measurement storage — capture and organize body measurements in a structured, searchable database.
  • ✓
    AI-powered trend analysis — surface patterns in sizing data across individuals and collections.
  • ✓
    Personalized fit recommendations — get body-type aware suggestions for clothing and styling choices.
  • ✓
    Daily tips & onboarding — helpful nudges on measurements, fit, and app usage to improve the experience over time.
Built for tailors, fashion businesses, and individuals who want more than numbers on a page.

FAQ

Questions Answered.

Everything you want to know about Monoversal Hub and our products.

What is Monoversal Hub?
▾

Monoversal Hub is an independent product studio based in Omoku, Rivers State, Nigeria. We design and build practical, people-first digital tools for problems that most tech companies overlook. We are funded by community support — not investors — and we build because we genuinely care about the people using our products.

Are your products free to use?
▾

Our products are currently in development. When they launch, our focus will be on accessibility — especially for users in Nigeria and Africa. Pricing details will be announced closer to launch. For now, joining the waitlist is completely free and gives you early access.

Where is Monoversal Hub based?
▾

We are based in Omoku, Rivers State, Nigeria. We are a fully remote-first studio and our products are being built to serve people locally and globally. We are registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 with BN: 9319562.

What exactly is SOVR?
▾

SOVR is a community-powered emergency response platform. When a user is in trouble, they raise an SOS from their location, describe the type of emergency, and nearby users with the capacity to help are notified instantly. Victims can see available helpers in real time and choose the best option. Everyone on the platform is both a potential victim and a potential rescuer.

How does SOVR work without reliable internet?
▾

This is something we're actively designing for. Our goal is to ensure SOVR works reliably in low-bandwidth environments common across Nigeria and Africa. We're exploring offline functionality, SMS fallback, and lightweight data usage as part of the product design. More details will be shared as development progresses.

Who can use SOVR?
▾

SOVR is designed for three tiers of users: Individuals (personal users), Private Organizations (businesses, NGOs, community groups), and Public Organizations (government agencies, emergency services). Any user can be both a help-seeker and a help-giver depending on the situation.

What is FYTD and who is it for?
▾

FYTD is an AI-powered measurements and body fit intelligence tool. It stores body measurements, analyzes data trends, and gives personalized fit recommendations. It's built for tailors, fashion businesses, and individuals who want intelligent insights from their sizing data — not just a list of numbers.

Will FYTD support both male and female measurements?
▾

Yes. FYTD is being designed with separate measurement systems and fit logic for different body types. Our design accounts for the unique proportions and fit requirements across genders, ensuring recommendations are accurate and relevant for every user.
